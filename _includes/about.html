About

We would like to contribute to a smart society that enjoys healthy longevity through AI technology that supports training to make subjective skill up. To achieve this goal, we are developing an "AI smart coaching technology to promote voluntary skill enhancement" by using an small number of wearable sensors and individual skill grasping technology.

AI

Level setting based on estimation of physical effort, using a powerful ANN algorithm we will define the users performance to provide physical or support or opposite force to set the task level of the current exercise.

Motion Sensing

Wearable sensing and skill estimation by digital human model, realize real-time skill segment classification with a simple and small number of sensors, and visualizing the training effect.

Hardware Design

Development of artificial muscle training suits, using lightweight, flexible, and inexpensive low-pressure driven pneumatic artificial muscles (PAMs).

Using this three elements as fundamental base we will develop flexible and lightweight artificial muscle suits, training software with an intuitive user interface, and verify the effect for rehabilitation and special skills.

Toward smart society to enjoy healthy long life

We will contribute to a society that enjoys healthy longevity by improving independence of elderly and disabled people with technology that can be used at home, generating an extension of healthy life span by effective and efficient care for individual and reducing social security and medical expenses with securing manpower in aging society.
